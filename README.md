# Global Sales & Profit Performance Dashboard

## Business Overview

Organizations operating across multiple regions and product categories require continuous visibility into sales performance, profitability trends, and regional contribution to support strategic decision-making. This project develops an interactive Excel dashboard to analyze sales versus profit across product categories, regions, and months to improve business monitoring and performance evaluation.

---



---

## Business Problem

Large sales datasets often make it difficult for management to quickly identify:

- High-performing and low-performing regions
- Seasonal sales and profitability trends
- Product categories generating the highest returns
- Operational areas contributing most to revenue growth

Without a centralized reporting dashboard, organizations may experience delayed decision-making, inefficient regional performance tracking, and limited visibility into profitability drivers.

This project provides a structured analytical dashboard to improve financial reporting and business performance monitoring.

## Analytical Approach

### Data Preparation
- Reviewed the dataset for missing and null values
- Structured transactional sales data for dashboard reporting
- Organized month-wise and region-wise performance metrics

### Financial & Operational Analysis
- Calculated total sales and total profit across months and regions
- Compared profitability trends between geographic markets
- Evaluated product category contribution toward organizational revenue

### Dashboard Development
- Built dynamic summary tables for:
  - Month-wise sales vs profit
  - Region-wise sales vs profit
- Developed interactive category selection using combo box controls
- Created visual charts for performance comparison and trend analysis
- Applied Excel functions to automate calculations and reporting logic

---

## Key Business Insights

### Regional Performance
- Central region generated the highest sales and profit contribution across product categories
- South region followed as another strong revenue-generating market
- Emerging regions demonstrated lower profitability and may require operational review

### Monthly Trends
- Sales and profitability remained consistently strong throughout the year
- October and December reflected peak business performance periods
- Seasonal fluctuations were easier to identify through month-wise visualization

### Product Category Analysis
- Product category filtering improved management visibility into category-level performance
- Dashboard controls enabled quick comparison of profitability across categories

### Operational Monitoring
- Visual charts simplified identification of:
  - revenue concentration
  - profit-driving regions
  - sales trends
  - performance patterns
